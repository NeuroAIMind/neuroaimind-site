## Neurons to Mind. Brain to AI.

We bring together biology, psychology, neuroscience, and AI to uncover how brain–body systems generate emotion—and translate these insights into intelligent systems and biomedical technologies that advance mental health.

---

## The Challenge

We do not yet understand how neural and physiological activity gives rise to emotion—especially as it unfolds over time and across individuals.

Current approaches remain fragmented across levels of analysis—from cellular mechanisms to whole-brain and body dynamics—without a unifying framework that links mechanism to function. Modern AI can model behavior, but it does not explain how emotion emerges from biological systems or evolves in real-world environments.

As a result, we lack the ability to track, predict, and regulate emotional states, both within individuals and across social contexts. Real-world phenomena—from rapid shifts in collective behavior to persistent mental health disorders—highlight this gap.

This limitation constrains our ability to develop precise and personalized approaches to mental health, with current treatments often failing to account for individual differences in underlying neural and physiological mechanisms.

Bridging this gap requires integrating across scales, developing models that are both biologically grounded and computationally precise, and capturing the dynamic, interactive nature of emotional states.

## Our Approach

We study how emotion emerges and evolves across scales using a closed-loop framework that connects neuroscience and AI.

We investigate the cellular basis of emotion and how it arises from interactions within neural populations and their coupling with the body, forming structured, dynamic patterns of activity that encode emotional states over time.

By integrating neural, physiological, and behavioral data with computational modeling, we uncover the mechanisms governing these brain–body states. We formalize these mechanisms into models that can infer latent emotional states, predict their evolution, and simulate how they change under different conditions.

These models are not only descriptive but actionable: they enable closed-loop systems that can test hypotheses, guide interventions, and adapt in real time.

Building on these foundations, we develop tools and platforms to advance human mental health.

## Vision & Impact

Our goal is to establish a mechanistic and predictive foundation for understanding emotion.

By linking neural mechanisms to computational models, we aim to:

- Advance our understanding of how emotion emerges and evolves in the brain
- Enable intelligent systems that model and interact with human emotional states
- Enable new approaches to mental health

This work opens a path toward more interpretable, controllable, and human-aligned AI, and toward new ways to understand, predict, and treat conditions such as anxiety, depression, addiction, and neurodegenerative disease.

## About

NeuroAI Mind Labs is an independent nonprofit research organization working openly with the global scientific community.

Our mission is to bridge brain science and AI to enable a deeper understanding of human emotion and to advance mental health.

We welcome collaboration with researchers, clinicians, and partners who share this vision.
